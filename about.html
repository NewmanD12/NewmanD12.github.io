My Background

During my junior year of high school I had already made up my mind that I was going into the United States Air Foce. Three months after graduation, off I went to basic training. Coincidentally it was the first time I had ever been on an airplane also, talk about nervous!

After basic training, I then went to technical school for the job that I had picked in the Air Force, an avionics technician. I spent time in Mississippi and Northern Texas afterwhich I got orders to my first base, RAF Lakenheath. This is where the fun actually began, and I began my journey as an F-15 avionics technician.
